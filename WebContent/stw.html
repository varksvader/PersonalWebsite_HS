STW, also known as Scientific and Technical Writing, is closely related to STEM and is also taught by Ms. Curran. This class is where more of the writing comes in play. We do our STEM project, but to present it we have to create a poster board and write a paper.

In order to prepare us for it, Ms. Curran had us write a grant proposal. Our grant proposals were entered into a EngineerGirl Contest (Boys can participate too). My grant proposal is about the endangered Geoffrey Spider Monkeys. They live in Central America and are losing their habitats because of deforestation. I proposed that we create synthetic wood instead of cutting down trees. To look more into it, please read it here.

Because this is a science class, we also read a lot of science articles. After reading these articles, we often write short summaries on them. One of my summaries is about how computer AIs are nice, but they can develop bad habits with exposure.
